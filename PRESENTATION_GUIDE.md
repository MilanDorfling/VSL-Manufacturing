## 3. HOSTING & COSTS (5 minutes)

### A. Site Size & Requirements

**Deployed Site Size: 2.03 GB (2,083 MB)**

**Breakdown:**

- Videos: ~2 GB (3 video files)
  - VSLVID - Trim.mp4: 1,056 MB
  - VSL Footage - Press Pack1.mp4: 998 MB
  - laser_cut.mp4: 41 MB
  - Images: ~80 MB
  - Code/CSS/JS: ~550 KB
  - Other assets: ~200 KB

**Hosting Requirements:**

- **Storage**: At least 3-5 GB (site + buffer)
- **Bandwidth**: High (videos use significant bandwidth)
- **Important**: Each visitor watching videos = ~2 GB bandwidth used

### B. Recommended Hosting Options (South Africa)

**Option 1: Afrihost Business Hosting (RECOMMENDED)**

- **Price**: ~R150-R200/month
- **Storage**: 20-50 GB
- **Bandwidth**: Unlimited or very high
- **Benefits**:
  - Local SA servers (faster for SA visitors)
  - Reliable uptime
  - Good support
  - Sufficient for your needs

**Option 2: Xneelo (formerly Hetzner SA)**

- **Price**: ~R150-R200/month
- **Storage**: 20-50 GB
- **Benefits**: Good local support, reliable

**Option 3: HostAfrica**

- **Price**: ~R120-R180/month
- **Storage**: 20-40 GB
- **Benefits**: Established, good pricing

### C. Additional Considerations

**Video Hosting Alternative:**

- **Current**: Videos hosted on your site (2 GB)
- **Alternative**: Host videos on YouTube/Vimeo and embed them
  - **Saves**: Hosting costs and bandwidth
  - **Trade-off**: Videos on third-party platform
  - **Recommendation**: Keep on-site for professional appearance

**Domain Name:**

- Need to purchase: vslmanufacturing.co.za or similar
- Cost: ~R100-R200/year

**SSL Certificate:**

- Required for security (HTTPS)

**EmailJS:**

- **Current plan**: Free (200 emails/month)
