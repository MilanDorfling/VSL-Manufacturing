Message from the VSL Directors
“This facility is more than an engineering achievement — it is the result of shared belief, hard work, and enduring
partnerships. Forged in collaboration and built with purpose, it reflects our confidence in the people, potential, and future
of South African manufacturing.”
From the moment we founded VSL, our vision was clear — to build a proudly South African company capable of
world-class manufacturing excellence while uplifting the communities around us.
Today, that vision takes tangible form in the opening of our new Gqeberha facility — a R760 million investment in people,
technology, and opportunity. This milestone is more than an achievement; it is a symbol of what can be accomplished
when partnerships are forged with purpose and belief.
Every beam, press, and component within these walls reflects collaboration, resilience, and trust — the same values that
define our journey. Together with our partners, we have built not just a facility, but a foundation for the future of local
manufacturing.
